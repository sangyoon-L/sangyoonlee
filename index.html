Bio

Hi! I'm a Medical Physics Ph.D. student at the University of Minnesota, working under the guidance of Prof. Patrick Bolan.

My research interests lie in multimodal medical AI in radiation oncology and radiology. My recent works include image-to-image translations and regression problems using deep learning for automatic MR spectroscopy voxel placement and dose heterogeneity prediction in Gamma Knife radiosurgery.

Before starting a Ph.D. study, I was a Research Scientist at the Seoul Asan Medical Center under the supervision of Dr. Namkug Kim. I got my Bachelor of Arts in Physics from Grinnell College, mentored by Dr. Keisuke Hasegawa.
